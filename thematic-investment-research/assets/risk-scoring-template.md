## Scoring Scales

### Probability Scale
| Score | Definition | Likelihood |
|-------|------------|------------|
| 1 | Remote | <10% chance |
| 2 | Unlikely | 10-30% chance |
| 3 | Possible | 30-50% chance |
| 4 | Likely | 50-70% chance |
| 5 | Almost Certain | >70% chance |

### Impact Scale
| Score | Definition | Value Impact |
|-------|------------|--------------|
| 1 | Minimal | <5% impact |
| 2 | Minor | 5-10% impact |
| 3 | Moderate | 10-20% impact |
| 4 | Significant | 20-40% impact |
| 5 | Severe | >40% impact |

### Risk Score Matrix
|  | Impact 1 | Impact 2 | Impact 3 | Impact 4 | Impact 5 |
|--|----------|----------|----------|----------|----------|
| **Prob 5** | 5 | 10 | 15 | 20 | 25 |
| **Prob 4** | 4 | 8 | 12 | 16 | 20 |
| **Prob 3** | 3 | 6 | 9 | 12 | 15 |
| **Prob 2** | 2 | 4 | 6 | 8 | 10 |
| **Prob 1** | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |

### Score Interpretation
| Score Range | Category | Required Action |
|-------------|----------|-----------------|
| 1-4 | Monitor | Track; no immediate action |
| 5-9 | Plan | Develop contingency plans |
| 10-15 | Mitigate | Active risk reduction required |
| 16-25 | Critical | Reconsider position; urgent action |
