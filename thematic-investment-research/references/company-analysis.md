## Business Quality Assessment

### Competitive Moat Analysis

**Network Effects**
- Does value increase with scale/users?
- Direct network effects (user-to-user value)
- Indirect network effects (ecosystem value)
- Data network effects (improvement with usage)

*Scoring*: Strong (3), Moderate (2), Weak (1), None (0)

**Switching Costs**
- Financial switching costs
- Procedural switching costs (learning, integration)
- Relational switching costs (trust, relationships)

*Evidence*: Customer retention rates, contract lengths, implementation complexity

**Intangible Assets**
- Brand recognition and premium pricing
- Patents and proprietary technology
- Regulatory licenses and certifications
- Proprietary data assets

*Evidence*: Brand value rankings, patent citations, regulatory barriers

**Cost Advantages**
- Economies of scale
- Process innovation/efficiency
- Geographic/resource advantages
- Learning curve benefits

*Evidence*: Margin comparison vs. peers, cost per unit trends

**Efficient Scale**
- Market size limits new entrants
- Natural monopoly/oligopoly dynamics
- High fixed cost / low marginal cost structure

*Evidence*: Market concentration, capacity utilization, new entrant failures
